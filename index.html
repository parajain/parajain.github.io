About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Edinburgh. My advisor is Prof. Mirella Lapata and my research area is natural language processing. I’m also a part of the CDT in NLP. I graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology, Hyderabad with an M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering where I was advised by Prof. Vineeth N Balasubramanian. In 2012, I graduated from Jabalpur Engineering College with a B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering.
